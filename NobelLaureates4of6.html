Welcome to the Laureate Explorer

This application provides an interactive way to delve into the lives and achievements of Nobel Peace Prize laureates from 1975 to 1993, based on the provided research report. You can explore each laureate's biographical details, their defining contributions to peace, and a unique prognostication on the potential for Artificial Intelligence (AI) to replicate their abilities and lived experiences. Use the filters to navigate by year, or browse the full list. Click on any laureate to view their detailed profile. A comparative chart also visualizes the AI replication probability scores.

AI Replication Probability Analysis

This chart visualizes the "Lived Experience Replication Probability" scores assigned in the report. This speculative score (0-100%) estimates the likelihood that an AI could replicate the laureate's lived experiences or the unique wisdom, conviction, and moral authority derived from those experiences. A lower score suggests a greater reliance on uniquely human qualities.
